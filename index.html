The Team

Industry veterans, making the games
we'd rather be playing.

After more than two decades building big corporate live-service titles, we walked away to make games we ourselves want to play as lifelong strategy and tabletop fans.

We're a team of two-and-a-half, backed by a trusted handful of freelancers. Small by design, every decision passes through people who genuinely love this genre.

We're decades-deep players of TTRPGs, CRPGs, management and strategy games. The hex map is where the tabletop and the strategy screen meet, so that's what we named the studio.

Our goal is simple: make a game that makes you say "just one more turn" at midnight… and then keeps you playing four more hours until morning.
